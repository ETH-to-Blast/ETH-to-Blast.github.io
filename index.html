ETH to Blast Troubleshooting: Pending, Missing, Wrong Network

“My ETH bridge transfer is pending / stuck”

  • Source chain congestion: your tx isn’t confirmed yet → check tx hash on the source explorer.
  • Gas too low: speed up / replace if your wallet supports it.
  • Bridge provider delay: follow the explorer trail and any provider status indications.

“Bridge says completed but I don’t see ETH”

  • You’re on the wrong network in your wallet (switch to Blast Mainnet).
  • Wallet UI caching: refresh, restart, or re-add the network.
  • Check destination receipt in a Blast explorer — that’s the source of truth.

“I sent to the wrong address / wrong wallet account”

  • Confirm the receiving address on the explorer receipt.
  • If it’s not your address, recovery may not be possible — always test first.
Best debugging method: explorers first, UI second. Anchor everything on tx hashes and receipts.
